The shift

From an operations platform to an operations command center.

Construction doesn't fail on the build. It fails in the gaps — the decision made in a site meeting that never got written down, the defect photographed and forgotten, the approval buried in an email thread.

The hard parts are already built: voice and email become AI-generated records; issues, RFIs, approvals and contracts-with-embeddings all live in one system. What comes next isn't more features bolted on — it's the structural spine that turns a platform into a command center: one place where AI understands everything on the way in, shows you only what needs a human, proposes the next move, and can reconstruct any decision exactly as it stood the day it was made.

01

Capture anywhere

A voice note from site, a photo over Telegram, a recorded client meeting, an email, a QR scan — every channel lands in one pipeline, already understood.

02

One AI brain

Transcribe, classify, extract, cross-check against the contract, rank by urgency, and recommend the next action — automatically, the moment work arrives.

03

Total recall

An append-only event spine and knowledge graph mean every decision, change and approval is queryable forever — and the system gets sharper each time you correct it.
